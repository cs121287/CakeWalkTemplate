About Us

Our Story

Cake Walk Baking Co. was founded in 2015 with the simple goal of bringing joy through delicious baked goods. What started as a small passion project has grown into a beloved local bakery.

Our team of talented bakers combines traditional techniques with innovative flavors to create memorable treats for all occasions. From weddings to birthdays, our custom creations have been part of countless special moments.

Our Team

Led by our head pastry chef Elizabeth Chen, our team brings decades of combined experience to every creation. We're passionate about quality ingredients, exceptional taste, and beautiful presentation.
